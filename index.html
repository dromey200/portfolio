About Me

As a Senior UX Specialist and an Innovative UX Specialist, Conversational & AI Agent Design Leader, I am passionate about transforming user interactions and crafting intuitive digital experiences that truly delight users and drive business goals.

With a strong background in leading conversational and experience design, I thrive on solving complex problems and translating user needs into intuitive, engaging, and highly reliable solutions. My approach is deeply user-focused, driven by empathy and a comprehensive understanding of human behavior. I believe in continuous learning and staying ahead of industry trends to deliver cutting-edge solutions, particularly in the evolving landscape of AI agent development.

My core expertise includes:

  • User Experience (UX) Design: User Research, Wireframing, Prototyping, Usability Testing, UX Writing.
  • AI & Conversational Design: Specializing in AI Design and Conversational Design.
  • Tools & Methodologies: Proficient in Figma, Adobe XD, Miro, Storytelling, and Workshop Facilitation.

I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations where I can leverage my skills to create impactful digital products. Feel free to connect with me to explore how I can contribute to your team's success in transforming user interactions.
