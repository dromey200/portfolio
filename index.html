Featured Case Study

Horadric AI: The 48-Hour Pivot

How I used real-time analytics to diagnose an 80% bounce rate and re-engineered the onboarding flow to drive product-led growth.

The Challenge

Upon soft-launching "Horadric AI" (a Gemini-powered loot analyzer), I drove 280+ users in 24 hours. However, analytics revealed a critical friction point: users were hitting the "API Key" login wall and bouncing in < 3 seconds.

The Strategy

  • Removed the Gate: Implemented a "Golden Path" Demo Mode allowing users to experience value instantly without authentication.
  • Context-Aware Logic: Upgraded the LLM prompt to detect "Cross-Class" utility (e.g., identifying items valuable for trading even if unusable by the player).
  • Tech Stack Pivot: Migrated from OpenAI to Gemini 2.5 Flash to eliminate server costs while increasing speed.
